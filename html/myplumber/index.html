Key Findings & Actions
🚨 Critical — Estimates
96.1% of 125,866 open estimates have zero follow-up contacts — $329M in pipeline that has never been touched after the initial estimate. Average open estimate age is 359 days. Even a 2% recovery on estimates under 1 year old = $2.6M incremental revenue.
🚨 Critical — Inventory
$7.35M in "unreceived" purchase orders, with 99.2% over 180 days old. Ferguson Enterprises ($3.25M) and Carrier Enterprise ($2.08M) account for 72% of exposure. Almost certainly phantom POs — goods received but never marked in ServiceTitan.
⚠️ Attention — Jobs
973 completed jobs show negative gross margin. The majority are small-dollar Plumbing and HVAC jobs where labor exceeded revenue. Review pricing floors, minimum service charges, and whether tech incentive structures are inadvertently driving overruns.
⚠️ Attention — Memberships
Membership export only contains canceled records. A full re-export is needed to assess current active member count, ARR, and renewal pipeline. Of the 1,436 cancellations, 31% cite non-payment — suggesting missed billing retry automation.
✅ Strength — Gross Margin
67.5% gross margin is the highest in the Sila portfolio so far. Sewer service is exceptionally profitable (Sewer Service: 98.7% GM, Sewer Installation: 92.1% GM). HVAC Installation at 49.3% is the only laggard — typical for install work but worth monitoring.
✅ Strength — Jobs Volume
71,853 completed jobs in 2 years across a diverse multi-trade operation. Stale job count is just 54 — an exceptionally clean open job queue for a business of this size. Peter Archuleta ($7.5M) and Hooman Khazei ($4.9M) are standout performers.
