⚠️ Critical Finding: Phantom PO Exposure
99.2% of all open PO value ($7.31M of $7.35M) is over 180 days old. This strongly indicates these are phantom POs — goods that were physically received but never marked as received in ServiceTitan. The Exported status dominates (98.8% of line items), meaning these POs were sent to vendors but never closed out after delivery.
Ferguson Enterprises ($3.25M) and Carrier Enterprise ($2.08M) account for 72.1% of open PO value. Focus reconciliation efforts with these two vendors first. Ferguson's volume suggests this is largely routine plumbing supply that has been received and consumed.
