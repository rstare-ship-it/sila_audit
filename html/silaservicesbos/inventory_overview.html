⚠️ Phantom PO Assessment
99.9% of open PO value ($19.06M of $19.1M) is over 180 days old. With 72% over 1 year old, these are almost certainly goods that were received but never marked as received in ServiceTitan. This is a critical data hygiene issue — the inventory and AP records do not reflect reality. A receiving audit is needed to close out legitimate POs.
The pattern is identical to other Sila portfolio companies (My Plumber: 99.2% >180 days, $7.35M). This is likely a systemic practice across the organization of not completing the receiving workflow in ServiceTitan after physical delivery.
