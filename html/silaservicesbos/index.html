Key Findings & Actions
🚨 Critical — Stale Jobs
1,685 stale open jobs — the highest in the Sila portfolio. 188 are over 1 year old, and the oldest is a Sales job open 843 days. This is likely a mix of stale estimates-to-jobs, projects in limbo, and scheduling backlogs. An immediate audit of the open job queue is needed to close, cancel, or reschedule.
🚨 Critical — Estimates Follow-Up
95.4% of 45,666 open estimates have zero follow-up contacts recorded. $540.6M in pipeline that has never been touched since the initial estimate. The SB-Sales BU alone holds $287M across 11,115 estimates. Even a 2% close rate on estimates under 1 year = $5.6M incremental revenue.
🚨 Critical — Inventory
$19.1M in unreceived POs with 99.9% over 180 days old. Top vendors are Distributor Corp of NE ($7.96M) and F.W. Webb ($5.15M). The pattern is identical to My Plumber — goods received in-field but the receiving step never completed in ServiceTitan. A vendor confirmation audit and PO write-off is needed.
⚠️ Attention — Negative Margin Jobs
6,046 completed jobs show negative gross margin. However, the majority are warranty BUs (1 Year Warranty at -3,691% GM, Multi Year Warranty at -15.7% GM) — this is expected cost absorption behavior, not operational failure. The approximately 300 non-warranty negative-margin jobs in HVAC and Plumbing Service warrant a pricing floor review.
⚠️ Attention — Membership ARR Mix
Of 8,837 active members, 7,821 (88.5%) are warranty/install products with no recurring revenue. True ARR is $344.7K from only 1,016 recurring members. The 18,265 expired memberships and warranty-holder base represent a large conversion opportunity if cross-sold to recurring Comfort Club plans at service touchpoints.
✅ Strength — Revenue Scale
$97.2M revenue over 2 years makes Sila Services BOS the largest brand in the portfolio by revenue. 62,386 completed jobs across HVAC, plumbing, and electrical. The HVAC Installation RREP BU is the revenue engine — $44.8M (46% of total) driven by a dedicated replacement/upgrade sales team.
