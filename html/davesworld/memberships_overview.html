🔍 Key Finding: Dave's World Uses Memberships Primarily for Warranty Tracking

Unlike a typical membership program, ~8,300 of Dave's World's 8,558 "active" memberships are product warranties — not paying service subscriptions. These appear as "z[Division] Imported Warranty" and "Coil to Coil Warranty" membership types, with $0 billing templates and no billing history.

This explains why 8,499 active memberships appear to have "never been billed" — they're not meant to be billed. The actual recurring-revenue membership program (Standard Comfort Club) is much smaller, with approximately ~200–300 enrolled paying members across all divisions.

This is an important distinction when benchmarking against other Sila brands. Dave's World's membership module is serving double duty as a warranty registry and a service plan tracker.

✅ Very Low Cancellation Rate

With only 29 cancellations out of 9,390 memberships (0.3%), Dave's World has essentially zero churn in its warranty and membership portfolio. This is a positive signal for customer retention and satisfaction — though it also reflects the nature of product warranties, which are rarely "cancelled" by customers.
