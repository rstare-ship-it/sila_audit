⚠ $9.5M Aged 181–365 Days

49% of all unreceived PO value is between 6 months and 1 year old. At this age, the materials have almost certainly been received and used on jobs — these POs likely need to be manually received or voided in ServiceTitan. Leaving them open inflates open liabilities and distorts job costing.

💡 Year-Old POs: Likely Already Used

POs dated 370+ days ago are almost certainly reconciled in the real world but never updated in ServiceTitan. Recommend batch-receiving and closing all POs older than 180 days in coordination with Homans and F.W. Webb — these two vendors account for 70% of unreceived value.
