⚠️ Attention — Imported Default BU Data Artifact
11,263 completed jobs under "Imported Default Businessunit" show $8.4M revenue at exactly 100% gross margin — indicating these are legacy-imported jobs with no cost data populated. This inflates overall portfolio GM. True operational GM excluding this BU is approximately 48–50%. All legacy imports should be reviewed for cost data completeness.
⚠️ Attention — Outstanding Balance
$1.60M outstanding across 652 completed jobs. Completed work that has not been fully collected. This is the second-largest outstanding balance in the portfolio. A systematic AR follow-up on these 652 accounts is a high-probability collection opportunity — the work has already been delivered.
⚠️ Attention — HVAC Service Margins
BF-HVAC Service at 36.3% GM and BF-Generator Installation at 34.5% GM are among the lowest in the T-Mark portfolio. Combined they represent $6.2M of revenue. A pricing floor review and technician productivity analysis for these two BUs is warranted — the service trade BUs in comparable brands routinely achieve 70%+ margins.
✅ Strength — Estimate Win Rate
33.8% win rate across 72,499 total estimates (24,493 sold at $77.0M) is among the strongest in the Sila portfolio. The BF-Sales-HVAC team is the primary revenue driver, with 8,969 HVAC Install jobs generating $42.6M. The foundation for follow-up-driven revenue growth is strong — the sales team clearly closes well when it engages.
✅ Strength — Clean Open Job Queue
Only 1 stale job detected (Job 291558, BF-Electrical Install, Hold, 54 days) out of 379 total open jobs. This near-clean queue across a 134,577-job dataset reflects strong scheduling discipline and job closure practices. The Hold/Scheduled/In Progress mix (50/267/62) is healthy and actively managed.
