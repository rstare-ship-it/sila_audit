🔍 Phantom PO Assessment Why open POs are almost always >180 days old
⚠️ Root Cause: Technicians receive materials at the supply house or at the job site, but the "Receive" step in ServiceTitan is not completed. The PO remains open indefinitely. This creates artificial inventory liability that doesn't reflect actual stock on hand.
💡 Resolution Path: (1) Pull the list of vendor invoices matching open POs from VP Supply, Irr Supply, and Home Depot. (2) Verify delivery against field records. (3) Mass-receive confirmed-delivered POs in ServiceTitan. (4) Write off any POs where goods were consumed but not trackable. VP Supply ($470K) and Irr Supply ($237K) should be the first audit targets.
