Section 1 — Open Purchase Order Summary
⚠️ Critical Finding: 99.2% of all open PO value ($10.0M of $10.1M) is on purchase orders that are over 180 days old. This is a significant AP hygiene issue — these POs are either already received and not marked as such in ServiceTitan, or represent genuine outstanding orders that are very overdue. Either way, they are distorting financial reporting and should be reconciled against actual vendor statements.
Context: This report covers Purchase Orders with status Exported (sent to vendor), Sent, or Partially Received — all indicating goods not yet fully received. The inventory data was exported in three overlapping date chunks (Mar 2024–Aug 2024, Aug 2024–Jun 2025, Jun 2025–Mar 2026); 205 duplicate lines were removed during combination.
