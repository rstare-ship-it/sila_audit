About This Portal

About Sila Brands

Sila Brands is a home services platform company operating multiple residential HVAC, plumbing, water treatment, and sewer service brands across the northeastern United States. All brands operate on ServiceTitan and share operational best practices across the portfolio.


This portal provides standardized operational intelligence reports for each brand — enabling consistent performance benchmarking and data-driven decision making across the portfolio.

Report Definitions

  • 📋 Jobs — Job costing, gross margin by BU, stale/unclosed jobs, outstanding balances
  • 📦 Inventory — Unreceived purchase orders by age, vendor, and business unit
  • 📝 Estimates — Open estimate pipeline, follow-up gaps, sold-but-unbooked work
  • 🏷️ Memberships — Active member health, billing anomalies, cancellation trends
